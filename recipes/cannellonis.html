Cannelllonis

Well done cannellonis

The cannellonis are pasta food with meat. It is the same of lasagna with the exception that is rolled.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Ricotta
  • Shredded cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Filling
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Pre-cook the pasta
  4. Fill the Cannelloni
  5. Assemble the dish
  6. Bake
  7. Serve
